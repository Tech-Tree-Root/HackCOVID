Scotty Shaw (Duke University, 2009) serves as the European Commission's #EUvsVirus CTO and the National Curator of the United States.


His journey towards becoming the global expert started with HackDuke, the premier hackathon for social impact and Duke University's flagship event.


Since early 2017, he has been the Founder/CEO/President of Tech Tree Root, an #EUvsVirus Initiator and the National Hackathon Organization of the United States.


Connect with Scotty Shaw online  >
As a registered Public Charity Organization of the United States, Tech Tree Root creates innovation ecosystems by generating opportunities and resources for technological and entrepreneurial education.

In 18 months, Scotty transformed the South Central United States innovation desert by launching hackathons and training organizers for every major school in Texas, Oklahoma, and Arkansas.

After educating 25,000+ students and mentoring many winners through annual events, Tech Tree Root expanded operations as far as California and Iceland, then led America's fight against coronavirus.

HackCOVID launched before America's coronavirus national emergency declaration to bring everyone a sense of hope and purpose in the impending confusion.


By recruiting talented designers and engineers to defeat coronavirus every week, it quickly became the national anti-coronavirus hackathon of the United States.


Connect with HackCOVID online  >
After four HackCOVIDs, the European Commission invited Scotty to join the #EUvsVirus team and help initiate a mission-driven initiative (Hackathon + Matchathon + EIC COVID Platform).

#EUvsVirus set world records by hosting 30,000+ participants and 2,164 international teams for the Hackathon, which ended with a jury of experts selecting 120 winning solutions.

Next, at the Matchathon, the #EUvsVirus team paired the winners with 500+ public and private entities to develop a world-record 2,235 new partnerships and put life-saving innovations into production.

Now, Scotty is rallying the world to save our future by building a global innovation ecosystem.
